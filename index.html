Decide with the world.

Pickle makes it easy to share and vote on decisions.

Coming soon for iOS.

Create.

Create your decision with a description and up to five choices. Each choice can have a caption and either a picture or video.

Share.

Share your decision with your followers for fast and easy feedback, and see decisions in your timeline from the people who you follow.

Vote.

Receive votes for the choices in your decision, and scroll through your timeline to vote on your friends' decisions. No one can see what you have voted for, so don't be scared to be honest!
